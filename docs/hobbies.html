Chris's Many hobbies

Snowboarding is a fantastic winter time release

I have been snowboarding for four years, but started skiing when I was four. I really enjoy the flow of snowboarding although I am a much better skiier. There's not quite anything like a deep powder day.

Soccer has been a huge staple

I have played soccer since I was in kindergarten, starting out playing rec. That soon developed into a competitive career that saw me promoted from C teams to A teams contesting state championships as well as regional tournaments. I still play casually to this day but two knee injuries have slowed me down.

Gaming is where I learned to vent rage into my desk

I have enjoyed gaming for a long time, the first game I got into was minecraft, but soon after titles like Clash of Clans, Dota 2, and CS:GO followed. I am only competitive in Dota 2, where I am in the top 80% of players worldwide.
